Did you know...

William Grimes is known by scholars as one of the most rebellious voices of the slave narratives. His book was written about eight years before the American Anti-Slavery Society and their involvement in the production of Slave Narratives. The Life of William Grimes, the Runaway Slave is the first fugitive slave narrative in American history.
